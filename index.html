Creator's

Heuristics

I've developed 10 guidelines, for your consideration, on how to make more responsibly.

1. If autonomy over my own creation stops at the first encounter, what does that mean for my social, physical, and conceptual body?

2. How do I mediate the process of semantic negotiation by balancing ambiguity and signification?

3. To what extent does my creation correlate spectators to domains of knowledge, types of normativity, and their own self?

4. Where is my creation taking spectators, for how long are they away, and where do they actually end up?

5. How will spectators change the creation and how will it change them?

6. How do I bring about the greatest degree of the most appropriate type of beauty and value in each situation possible?
